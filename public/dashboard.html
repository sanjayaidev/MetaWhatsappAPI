01 Getting Started

Set up your account and connect your WhatsApp number.

👤 Create your account
Go to Account → Connected Numbers and click Connect Number. Paste in your WhatsApp Business Account ID and a System User access token from Meta Business Manager, then select your number and connect.

📱 Connect your WhatsApp Business Number
Go to Account → Connected Numbers and click Connect Number. A Meta login popup will open — log in with the Facebook account linked to your WhatsApp Business Account and follow the steps. Your number will appear in the list once connected.

02 Creating Message Templates

Templates must be approved by Meta before you can send them.

⚠️ WhatsApp requires all bulk messages to use pre-approved templates. You cannot send free-form messages in bulk — only template messages are allowed.

📝 Open the Template Builder
Go to Templates in the sidebar and click + New. The template builder opens on the left with a live WhatsApp preview on the right.

🏷️ Fill in Basic Info
Enter a Template Name (lowercase, underscores only — e.g. welcome_message), choose a Category, and select a Language.

🖼️ Add a Header (Optional)
Choose from None, Text, Image, Video, or Document. For media headers, upload a file. This appears at the top of the WhatsApp message bubble.

💬 Write the Body
Type your message in the Body field. Use {{1}}, {{2}} etc. for personalization (e.g. contact name). Supports bold, italic, and strikethrough. The preview updates live as you type.

🔘 Add Buttons (Optional)
Add buttons below the message. Buttons appear under the preview.

🚀 Submit for Review
Click Submit for Review. Meta typically reviews templates within a few minutes to a few hours. You'll see the status change from PENDING to APPROVED or REJECTED automatically.

03 Managing Contacts

Import your recipient list before creating campaigns.

📋 Paste CSV
Go to Contacts and paste contacts directly in the text area: Name, Phone, Message — one per line. Or add a header row first (e.g. name,phone,message,city) to include extra columns as custom fields, same as file import.

📁 Import from CSV or Excel
Upload a .csv or .xlsx file with columns: name, phone, message. Any other column (e.g. email, order_id, city) is kept as a custom field you can map to a template placeholder when creating a campaign.

💾 Save to Database
After importing, click Save to DB to permanently store contacts. Unsaved contacts are lost on refresh.

🚫 Important: Only message contacts who have explicitly opted in. Sending unsolicited messages violates WhatsApp policy and can get your number banned.

04 Running Campaigns

Send bulk messages to your contact list.

📣 Create a Campaign
Go to Campaigns, enter a campaign name, select an APPROVED template, map placeholders to contact fields, and choose contacts. Click Create Campaign.

🗂️ Map Placeholders
If your template has {{1}}, {{2}} variables, map each to a contact field. For example, {{1}} → Contact Name will personalize each message.

▶️ Start the Campaign
Once created, the campaign is in draft status. Click ▶ Start to begin sending. Messages are sent with a delay between each to avoid spam detection.

📊 Track Delivery
Go to Campaign Logs, select your campaign, and view per-message status: sent, delivered, read, or failed.

05 Settings & Auto-Reply

Configure sending limits and automated responses.

⏱️ Sending Limits
Set hourly and daily message limits to stay within WhatsApp's messaging tier. Configure min/max gap between messages to space out sends.

🤖 Auto-Reply
Enable auto-reply to automatically respond to incoming messages. Without a Groq key, your System Prompt text is sent as a static reply. Add a Groq API key to enable AI-powered dynamic replies.

🔑 Groq API Key (AI Replies)
Get a free Groq API key at console.groq.com. Add it in Account settings to power auto-reply responses.

07 Frequently Asked Questions

Why was my template rejected?
Meta rejects templates that are too promotional or contain prohibited content. Use clear language and avoid all-caps or unnecessary URLs.

Why are messages sending slowly?
WaBlast adds a delay between messages to protect your number from spam flags. This helps maintain your number's quality rating.

Can I send messages without a template?
No. WhatsApp Business API only allows template messages for outbound bulk messaging.

How many messages can I send per day?
Daily limits depend on your WhatsApp Business Account and number quality rating.

My campaign stopped mid-way, why?
Check Campaign Logs for errors — it can be token expiry, API limits, or delivery issues.

How do I disconnect my WhatsApp number?
Go to Account → Connected Numbers and click Disconnect next to your number.
